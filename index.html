How it works

1. Stores upload products

Local businesses list products, prices, and availability.

2. Customers place orders

Customers choose products and submit their order online.

3. We collect and transport

Orders are picked up from the stores and moved to the pickup point.

4. Customer collects

The customer picks up the order from the fixed delivery location.
